About

Hello there, I have 3 years of experience as a UX designer, but my journey begun with my interest in drawing and art that goes back to when I was a child. This interest led me to study computer graphics and multimedia at Bangkok University International. While there, I discovered UX design and found my passion. I have always been fascinated by the psychological aspect of UX design and enjoy doing the research and analysis that underpin effective design. While at Morphosis, I have worked on projects for G-Able, JP Morgan, and Singha, among other clients.
